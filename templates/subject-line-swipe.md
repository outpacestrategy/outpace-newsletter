# Subject Line Swipe File

Formulas that work for this audience. Under 50 chars. Specific beats vague.

## Fear/Risk Angle
- "Google's update is killing AI content"
- "Your competitors are in AI search. You're not."
- "This SEO mistake is costing you rankings"
- "AI won't cite your site. Here's why."

## Curiosity + Specific
- "The 2-minute fix that gets AI to cite you"
- "Why your About page is now your most important page"
- "What 47% of Google searches look like now"
- "The format AI loves (and most sites skip)"

## Trend/News Hook
- "Google just changed the rules again"
- "Beehiiv added something worth knowing about"
- "Apple Maps is about to get more expensive"
- "ChatGPT is sending traffic now. Here's where."

## Contrarian
- "Stop writing blog posts. Do this instead."
- "More AI content isn't the answer"
- "Your website ranking means less than you think"

## Rules
- No emojis in subject lines
- No ALL CAPS
- Under 50 characters
- Always deliver on what the subject promises
